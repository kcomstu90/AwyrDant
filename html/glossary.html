Glossary

Notable Elves

Baron Calci

Leader of the Elves, Baron Calci is a respectable leader who adopts a no-nonsense approach to ruling. The forests belong to the Elves and the Dwarves have no right to their sacred groves. By right of being Firstborn, the wishes of the Elves must trump all others.


Calcior

Captain of the Guard in Avanyarci. Accused the Party of being spies for the dwarves; gave them the choice to turn on the dwarves or be executed.


Heirs of the Sky

Elven Order of Paladins that defeated a necromancer on T'sa Cbili before the war started. Attempted to remain neutral in the war but were largely unsucessful.


Notable Dwarves

Mab Corrach

Son of Tad Corrach, former king of Corrachod.

The execution of his brother, Plentyn, spurred him into all-out war with the Elves. One of his first attacks was against the forward positioned outpost of Avanyarci.


Moradin's Hammer

Dwarven Order of Paladins that was founded to investigate heinous acts committed by the Heirs of the Sky. Current members are still largely distrustful of the Heirs.


Tad Corrach

Former king of Corrachod. Presumably died in Baron Cacli's attack against Corrachod.


Notable Places

Avanyarci

A forward elven outpost, recently bolstered by Terori Sulis (elven elite guard), in anticipation of a dwarven attack.


Awyr Dant/T'sa Cbili

Also known as T'sa Cbili by the elves, it is the site of centuries of war between the archetypal enemies - Elves and Dwarves. Surrounded by hundreds of square miles of forest the elves hold so dear, yet, at its core, solid rock that no sane dwarf would ever give up. Long have armies fought on its slopes, blood running in thick rivulets down its broad slopes. Heroes were born and legends died fighting for their cause in vast groves and caverns.


Corrachod/Samot'cis

Corrachod was chief among Dwarven cities on Awry Dant. It was razed by Baron Calci after nearly two centuries of war.

Samot'cis was built over the same area as Corrachod after Corrachod was razed to the ground by Baron Calci.
